This is a simply designed but very interesting game.

You participate in the game to fight and destroy invading enemy aircraft. You will receive "Gold Coins", then use "Gold Coins" to upgrade the plane.

Upgrading a plane is simply combining 2 level 1 planes into a level 2 plane, combining 2 level 2 planes into a level 3 plane, etc., similar to upgrading a plane to a higher level. .

The higher the plane's upgrade, the stronger it will be when fighting and shooting down enemy planes will receive more "Gold Coins".

Additionally, you can buy planes directly from the "Feed" section using $Plane.
You can buy $Plane with TON through @wallet's Wallet Pay.
